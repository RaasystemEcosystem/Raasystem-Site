How Raasystem Works

At its core, Raasystem blends blockchain, AI, and real-world assets to deliver efficient, scalable, and decentralized finance and commerce solutions across Africa and beyond.

Core Architecture

XDC Blockchain Backbone

All smart contracts and tokens run on the XDC Network, ensuring low fees and fast finality.

Raaskoin & Oracle

Raaskoin is pegged to live gold prices via a custom Chainlink-fed oracle synced on-chain.

Raastoken Governance

Utility and governance token powering DAO decisions, staking, and ecosystem incentives.

Modular Smart Contracts

Plug-and-play contracts for payments, lending, farming, and tokenization.

AI Trading Engine

Dynamic Order Book

AI maintains optimal spreads based on market demand and liquidity availability.

Arbitrage Detection

Cross-exchange AI scans for price disparities and executes profitable trades.

Liquidity Optimization

Rebalances pools in real time to reduce slippage and maximize yield.

Smart Market-Making

Adapts bid/ask spreads dynamically with minimal manual input.

User Interaction

Raaswallet

Non-custodial wallet for managing Raaskoin, Raastoken, and supported assets securely.

Raaspay

Fiat–crypto gateway for merchants, complete with settlement, KYC, and real-time conversion.

RABEX

Trade interface for crypto and stocks with AI-backed order matching and analytics.

USSD & Mobile

Access the ecosystem even without internet through secure mobile protocols.
